About Vibrant Voices

The Vibrant Voices Network/le Réseaux des voix vibrantes is a collaboration of non-partisan, not-for-profit, membership-based organizations representing Canadian seniors and retirees from coast to coast to coast. Our network represents 150,000 Ontario members in this year’s provincial election—keeping our members engaged and informed on party positions and the policy issues that are most relevant to seniors.
